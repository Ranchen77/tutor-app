How it works

Simple for kids,
powerful for parents

Each kid picks a subject, answers 10 questions, and gets instant results. Score 80%+ to earn rewards for that session.

Parents unlock the parent panel with a PIN to redeem screen time, add bonus minutes, or pay out allowance earnings.
